    • Break bulk Cargo: Unless different terms are specifically stated the quoted ocean freight rate is offered on load port crane hook to discharge port crane hook (Hook / Hook) terms. Any requirement for delivery of cargo to the wharf or ship's side is to be arranged by the cargo interest with the local agent at the time of booking.
